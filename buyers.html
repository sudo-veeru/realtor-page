Steps to Buying a Home

1. Get Pre-Approved for a Mortgage

+

Before you start house hunting, get pre-approved for a mortgage to know your budget and show sellers you’re a serious buyer.

2. Start Your Home Search

+

Begin your search by exploring different neighborhoods, comparing properties, and visiting open houses to find your perfect home.

3. Make an Offer

+

Once you’ve found a home you love, make an offer. Your real estate agent will help you with the negotiation process.

4. Conduct Home Inspections

+

Arrange for a home inspection to identify any potential issues with the property before finalizing the purchase.

5. Close the Deal

+

Finalize your mortgage, sign the paperwork, and close the deal to become the new owner of your home.
